Fruit Dip

Great with fresh fruit!

Servings: 24
Yield: 3 cups

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • ½ cup marshmallow creme
  • 2 cups frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • ¼ cup unsweetened pineapple juice

Directions

  1. Step 1 Blend together the cream cheese, marshmallow cream and thawed topping. Add enough pineapple juice to make it dipping consistency. Chill for 1 hour.
